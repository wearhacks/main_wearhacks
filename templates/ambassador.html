The Program

The WearHacks Leadership program offers our Ambassadors a chance to test their talents.

By building skills in leadership, networking and event management- Ambassadors complete our program feeling like experts in hacking hackathons!

The WearHacks City Ambassador is a volunteer position to be the leader of a WearHacks event. You will take charge of a core team of three to four people, and direct them into creating the best WearHacks event to date.
You learn new skills including team leadership, networking, and fundraising. We love to see you succeed, and we love it even more when we can help you achieve it.
